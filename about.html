About Baribaba

What is Baribaba?

Baribaba is a Kolkata-based broker-free room and flat finding platform that helps students, working professionals, and families connect directly with property owners — without paying heavy brokerage fees.

We focus on verified listings, transparent communication, and real human support to make house-hunting simple, affordable, and stress-free.

At Baribaba, we believe:

Finding a home should not come with unnecessary extra costs.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make rental housing affordable and accessible by eliminating brokerage fees and providing verified property listings with complete transparency.

Our Vision

To become Kolkata’s most trusted broker-free rental platform and expand across major cities in India — setting a new standard for stress-free home searching.

Why Baribaba Started

Baribaba was created to solve a common problem. Every year, thousands of people moving to Kolkata end up paying one month’s rent as brokerage — often for listings that are not verified or properly managed.

We asked a simple question:

Why should people pay extra just to find a room?

To change this system, Baribaba introduced a broker-free, digital-first model that connects tenants directly with property owners.

The Broker-Free Concept

  • ✔ No brokerage fees
  • ✔ No hidden charges
  • ✔ Direct owner communication
  • ✔ Verified property listings
  • ✔ Transparent process

Our broker-free concept ensures affordability, clarity, and faster decision-making for tenants.

Founder Story – Argha Chakraborty & Soumik Pramanik

Baribaba was founded by Argha Chakraborty and Soumik Pramanik with a shared vision of solving real-life rental problems in Kolkata.

After personally experiencing the challenges of paying brokerage and dealing with unreliable listings, they decided to build a smarter alternative.

With a focus on technology, trust, and transparency, they created Baribaba — a platform designed to serve real people with real needs.

Their goal is simple: Make house hunting easier, fairer, and more affordable.

Argha – Co-Founder

Argha is the driving force behind Baribaba’s mission to make rental housing transparent and broker-free. He believes that trust and technology together can transform the rental experience.

Soumik – Co-Founder

Soumik focuses on operations, verification, and customer support. His goal is to ensure every listing is genuine and every user receives quick, reliable assistance.

Their Shared Vision

If people can book flights and hotels online without agents, why not find a room without brokers?

Together, they are building Baribaba to become Kolkata’s most trusted rental solution — focused on transparency, affordability, and genuine support.
