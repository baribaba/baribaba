About BariBaba

Founded in 2025, BariBaba was created to remove the broker barrier from property searches in Kolkata. Our mission is simple: help students, professionals, and families find verified PGs, flats, and plots with transparent coordination and zero brokerage.

What We Offer
  • PG Listings & Coordination
  • Flats for Rent
  • Flats for Sale
  • Residential Plots
  • Rent agreements and owner coordination
Why BariBaba?

We verify listings, talk to owners on your behalf, and ensure transparent deals that don't charge brokerage. Local support, fast responses, and honest coordination are our priorities.
