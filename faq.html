Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay brokerage to find a property through Baribaba?
No heavy brokerage! Baribaba focuses on connecting tenants and owners directly to reduce unnecessary middlemen charges.
How can I list my property on Baribaba?
Go to the "List Property" page, fill out the property details form, and submit. Our team will review and publish it.
Is Baribaba available only in Kolkata?
Currently, we are focused on Kolkata and nearby areas. Expansion to other cities is planned soon.
How can I contact the property owner?
You can directly call or message the owner via the WhatsApp button available on each property page.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes. We respect your privacy and do not share your personal information without consent.
How quickly will I receive responses to my inquiry?
Most owners respond within a few hours. However, response time may vary depending on availability.
