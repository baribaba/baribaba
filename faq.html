Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Baribaba

Common Questions

1. What is Baribaba?
Baribaba is a broker-free room and flat finding platform in Kolkata that helps tenants connect directly with property owners. We provide verified rental listings without charging heavy brokerage fees.
2. Is Baribaba completely broker-free?
Yes. Baribaba focuses on a broker-free rental model where tenants can find rooms and flats without paying traditional one-month brokerage charges.
3. How does Baribaba verify rental listings?
We aim to provide verified room and flat listings by checking property details before publishing. This helps reduce fake listings and improves transparency for tenants.
4. What types of properties are available on Baribaba?
You can find single rooms, shared rooms, PG accommodations, 1BHK, 2BHK & family flats — all focused within Kolkata’s rental market.
5. Is Baribaba only available in Kolkata?
Currently, Baribaba primarily operates in Kolkata. Our goal is to expand to other major cities in India in the future.
6. Do tenants have to pay any hidden charges?
No. Baribaba believes in transparency. We do not promote hidden brokerage fees or surprise commissions.
7. How can property owners list their property on Baribaba?
Property owners can collaborate with Baribaba by submitting their property details through our listing process. Once reviewed, the property can be added to our platform for tenant visibility.
8. Is Baribaba safe for students moving to Kolkata?
Yes. Baribaba is designed especially to help students and working professionals find verified rooms and PG accommodations without brokerage pressure.
9. How is Baribaba different from traditional brokers?
Traditional brokers usually charge one month’s rent as commission. Baribaba reduces dependency on middlemen by enabling direct communication between tenants and owners through a transparent system.
