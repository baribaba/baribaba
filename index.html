About Baribaba

Founded in 2025 by Argha Chakraborty and Soumik Pramanik, Baribaba is your friendly neighborhood real estate platform designed to make property dealings simple, safe, and stress-free. Whether you are looking for a plot, a flat to rent, or a flat to buy, we bring all your options together in one place so you don’t have to run from one broker to another.

We believe in our tagline — “From Amar to Tomar – Baribaba is Shobar” — meaning we are here for everyone. Our goal is to connect people with the right property, at the right price, with complete transparency.

At Baribaba, we take away the confusion that usually comes with buying or renting property. Every listing is checked, details are clear, and the process is straightforward — so you can focus on finding your dream place while we handle the rest.

Why People Choose Baribaba:

  • Easy to use: A simple website that anyone can use without tech skills.
  • Verified properties: No fake or misleading listings.
  • Wide choice: From small plots to luxury flats — we have it all.
  • Transparent deals: No hidden charges, no surprises.
  • Friendly help: Our support team is always here for you.

Our Vision:

To make property search as easy as online shopping — quick, reliable, and stress-free for everyone in India.
