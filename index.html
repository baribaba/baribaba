About Us | Baribaba

Baribaba – From Amar to Tomar, Baribaba is Shobar

Baribaba is Kolkata’s trusted real estate coordination platform, founded by Argha Chakraborty and Soumik Pramanik. Our mission is to simplify property transactions by connecting buyers, sellers, and renters directly, eliminating unnecessary intermediaries, and making real estate transparent, efficient, and secure.

We offer a wide range of services, including verified residential and commercial property listings, hassle-free buying, selling, and renting, and personalized support for every client. Every property listed on Baribaba is verified, ensuring genuine inquiries and smooth transactions.

At Baribaba, we believe a property is more than a space—it’s where life happens, businesses grow, and memories are made. Our platform is designed to save time, reduce stress, and provide a reliable experience for anyone looking to navigate Kolkata’s property market.

Whether you are searching for your dream home, planning a property investment, or looking for a rental space, Baribaba is your go-to partner. Join thousands of satisfied clients who trust us for safe, seamless, and smart real estate solutions in Kolkata.
