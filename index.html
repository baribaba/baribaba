About Us | Baribaba

From Amar to Tomar, Baribaba is Shobar

Baribaba is Kolkata’s trusted real estate coordination platform founded by Argha Chakraborty and Soumik Pramanik.

We simplify renting, buying, selling, and managing properties by connecting owners, tenants, buyers, and agents through verified listings and transparent coordination.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Baribaba?
Baribaba is a real estate coordination platform that connects property owners, tenants, buyers, and agents.
Is Baribaba a broker?
No. Baribaba works as a coordination and support platform to ensure transparency and smooth communication.
Can I post my property for free?
Yes, property posting is free. Premium support services may include charges.
Which locations do you serve?
Currently we focus on Kolkata and nearby regions, expanding soon.
How can I contact Baribaba?
You can contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or email mentioned on the website.
