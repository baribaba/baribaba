About Us | Baribaba

From Amar to Tomar, Baribaba is Shobar

Baribaba is Kolkata’s trusted real estate platform, founded by Argha Chakraborty and Soumik Pramanik. We simplify property buying, selling, and renting by connecting clients directly, making every transaction secure and hassle-free.

Our verified listings, personalized support, and transparent processes ensure a smooth experience for buyers, sellers, and renters alike. With Baribaba, finding or listing your property is fast, safe, and reliable.

Baribaba FAQ

1. What is Baribaba?
Baribaba is a modern property coordination platform that connects property seekers and owners seamlessly. Our motto is “From Amar to Tomar, Baribaba is Shobar”, meaning we make property services accessible to everyone.
2. How does Baribaba work?
Baribaba simplifies property buying, selling, or renting by coordinating between property owners, agents, and customers. You can post a property, search for properties, or request our team’s assistance directly through the platform.
3. Who can use Baribaba?
Anyone looking to buy, sell, or rent a property in Kolkata and surrounding areas can use Baribaba. Property owners, tenants, and real estate agents are all welcome to join our platform.
4. Is there a fee to use Baribaba?
Creating an account and browsing properties is free. Certain services, like premium listings or property coordination support, may have associated charges, which are clearly communicated before use.
5. How can I post my property on Baribaba?
You can post your property by filling out our “Post Property” form on the website. Provide details like property type, location, price, and images, and our team will review and list it promptly.
6. How can I contact Baribaba for support?
You can reach us via:
Email: help@baribaba.com
Phone: 8240473982
WhatsApp: Available for direct queries on our contact page
7. How quickly are properties verified or listed?
Our team usually reviews and lists properties within 24–48 hours. Verification ensures the authenticity and quality of each listing.
8. Is Baribaba only for Kolkata?
Currently, Baribaba primarily operates in Kolkata and nearby areas. We plan to expand to other cities soon.
9. Can Baribaba help with property legalities?
While Baribaba is a coordination platform, we provide guidance and connect you with trusted professionals for legal documentation, property verification, and RERA compliance.
10. Who founded Baribaba?
Baribaba was founded by Argha Chakraborty and Soumik Pramanik, with the mission of making property services simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone.
11. What makes Baribaba different from other real estate platforms?
Unlike traditional property portals, Baribaba focuses on coordination and transparency. We ensure that users get verified listings, personalized support, and a hassle-free property experience.
12. How can I stay updated on new listings and offers?
You can subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or enable notifications on the website to receive updates on the latest properties and offers.
